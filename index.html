The Problem

Farmers Are Being Left Behind

Local sugarcane farmers in Western Visayas face a deeply inefficient system. Without digital infrastructure, they are at the mercy of delayed information, manual processes, and unpredictable market disruptions.

⏰

Delayed Price Information

Farmers receive mill gate price updates too late to make informed decisions about when and where to sell their harvest. By the time they know prices have dropped, it's already too late to delay delivery.

🚗

Long-Distance Travel for Payment

Farmers must travel long distances to sugar centrals and wait in line for hours just to receive their quedan documents and collect payment, losing an entire workday in the process.

📦

No Market Foresight on Importation

Lack of data on sugar importation schedules causes farmers to unknowingly harvest during periods of excess supply and when imported sugar floods the market, local prices crash significantly.

🚛

Uncoordinated Truck Deliveries

Without scheduling, trucks from multiple farms arrive simultaneously at the central, creating massive congestion. Drivers wait for hours, burning fuel, wasting time, and reducing daily capacity.

📄

Paper-Based Quedan Records

All quedan documentation is done manually on paper — prone to loss, damage, forgery, and errors. Farmers have no easy way to verify or track the status of their batch once it leaves the farm.

💸

Delayed Cash Payments

Even after queuing for their quedan, farmers often wait days or weeks for their cash payment to be processed. This cash flow gap creates financial strain, especially for small-scale farming families.

The Solution

Five Core Features

A unified mobile application that replaces every inefficient touchpoint in the traditional sugarcane supply chain with a seamless digital experience.

01

📈

Real-Time Price Monitoring

Instant push notifications whenever mill gate prices fluctuate. Compare prices across multiple sugar centrals and make informed selling decisions from any location.

Live Updates

02

🎫

Digital Quedan Tracker

Every quedan is issued digitally. View batch details and real-time status — Processing → Ready for Release → Paid — directly in the app. No paper, no guessing.

Paperless

💳

E-Wallet & Bank Integration

Payment transfers directly to GCash, Maya, BPI, or UnionBank upon quedan release. No physical collection, no queuing for cash.

🚨

Importation Impact Alerts

Monitors government announcements and importation data to warn farmers in advance. Data-driven recommendations identify optimal harvest windows to avoid price crashes during oversupply periods.

Harvest Scheduling System
